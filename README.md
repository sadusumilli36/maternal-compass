# Problem

Georgia is currently facing a silent emergency: it is one of the most dangerous states in the U.S. to give birth. The crisis isn't just medical; it's geographic.

The Maternal Desert: Over 50% of Georgia’s 159 counties lack a single OB-GYN or labor and delivery provider.

# Our Solution: MaternalCompass

MaternalCompass: a data-driven decision platform that identifies high-risk maternal care gaps across Georgia, allowing you to experiment with the benefits of adding locations for new county health centers.

# Key Features

1. Interactive Mapping: A county-by-county visualization of Georgia using React-Leaflet, allowing users to see the maternal health landscape at a glance.
2. Hospital and County Insights: Clicking on any county or any hospital location pin pops up a deep-dive data panel with the most relevant and critical data.
3. Bed Allocation Simulator: Simulate adding OB beds at a new clinic location in a county to instantly show impact on risk level assessment. (e.g. if I add a center with 15 obstetric beds to Walton County, will the risk level go down?)
5. AI Chatbot: Powered by the Gemini API with access to our county risk scores, hospital and OB bed data, prenatal care statistics, and more, the AI Assistant helps users understand the maternal health crisis in Georgia and answer their personalized questions.
