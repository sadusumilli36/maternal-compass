About MaternalCompass

MaternalCompass is a data-driven visualization tool designed to help families and healthcare professionals locate and understand the maternal care resources available across Georgia.

By integrating official maternal care facility listings with precise geographic data, this project aims to provide transparency into the different levels of care (I-IV) provided by Georgia's birthing centers.

Key Objectives

  • Accessibility: Pinpoint care facilities with accurate coordinates and addresses.
  • Education: Explain and visualize the Levels of Care (I through IV) to help families find the most appropriate facility.
  • Insight: Highlight county-level resource distribution to identify areas of high or low coverage.
